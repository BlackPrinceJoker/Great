Just Government

We exist to teach the principles of biblical government and empower Christians to reform politics.

What You Can Do

Believe

Believe that all authority comes only from God and that every ruler must submit to Christ's kingdom.

Repent

Repent of not submitting to the authority God has put over you and of misusing the power God has given you.

Pray

Pray for those in authority. Pray for righteous leaders to be blessed and for wicked leaders to be converted, and if not converted, then destroyed.

Support

Support the work by telling others about the project, displaying yard signs, and getting your church to publicly affirm the Just Government Principles.

Lead

If you are a man, run for public office or head endeavors to deepen and spread the knowledge of God in the earth.
