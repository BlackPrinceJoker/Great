The Just Government Project

We exist to teach the principles of biblical government and empower Christians to reform politics.

What You Can Do

Believe

Believe that all authority exists only by God's ordination and that every ruler is required to submit to Christ's domain.

Repent

Repent wherein you have not submitted to the authority God has placed you under and wherein you have abused the authority given to you by God.

Pray

Pray for those in authority. Pray for the righteous to prosper, for the wicked to be converted, and if not converted, then destroyed.

Support

Support the work by telling others about the project, displaying yard signs, and getting your church to publicly affirm the Just Government Principles.

Lead

If you are a man, run for public office or head endeavors to deepen and spread the knowledge of God in the earth.
