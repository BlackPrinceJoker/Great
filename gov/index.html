Just Government Principles

You are the salt of the earth. ‑ Matthew 5:13

[I]t does not bear the sword for nothing; for it is a minister of God, an avenger who brings wrath on the one who practices evil. - Ro 13:4

In this context, I’m using “abominations” to designate sins of individuals that bring God’s judgement upon their surrounding community.

When an abomination is committed, it is the government’s duty to take action that appeases God’s wrath against the community.

“I will require your lifeblood” - Ge 9:5
“no expiation can be made for the land for the blood that is shed on it, except by the blood of him who shed it” - Nu 35:33
“You shall not pity him, but you shall purge the blood of the innocent from Israel” De 19:13
“And the bloodguiltiness shall be forgiven them.” De 21:8
“So you shall purge the evil from Israel.” De 22:22

In the New Covenant, what sins are abominations, and what actions appease God’s wrath?
